[i]We have always had a good life in our homeworld, except one problem - we knew we did not originate from this planet. With no records of our origins found on our homeworld, we looked to the stars for answers. We hoped we would be welcomed among the stars, sharing our culture, our food, our way of life. Little did we know, we were the Original People, and the galaxy has no fond memories of our ancestors. The Fallen Empires have never forgotten or forgiven our crimes...[/i]

The Zenith of Fallen Empires: Original People presents an interesting start and scenario to your Stellaris game. This mod imagines that a species with unknown origins, searching the galaxy and looking for the clues to their mysterious past.  This is an add-on component mod to [url=http://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=1317022177]The Zenith of Fallen Empires 2.0[/url].
