[i]We were told the spirits created our world to perfection, made us in their image and placed us as stewards of all life on this world. Our primitive ancestors even thought the world was spherical. How laughable was that. I believe that someone built this massive structure - this Ringworld - or some thing. We were put here for a reason. But who would do such a thing, and what could they possibly want from us?[/i]

The Zenith of Fallen Empires: Ringworld Empires provides a number of starting systems for your stellar empire, perhaps hinting at your species' great yet mysterious past. This is an add-on component mod to [url=http://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=1317022177]The Zenith of Fallen Empires 2.0[/url] and note that this mod requires the base mod to run.
